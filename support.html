Make a Difference

Support Our Mission

Every contribution directly funds life-saving food, shelter, and medical support for internally displaced families in Karenni State. Your generosity keeps our teams in the field.

⚠ URGENT: Over 1,000 people were displaced by a military raid in Hpruso Township in May 2026. Emergency shelter and food support is critically needed right now.

Transparency

Where Your Money Goes

100% of donations go directly to field operations. Your contribution is allocated across three core intervention areas based on the most urgent needs on the ground.

Emergency Food Provisions

Funding standard rations distributed to IDP camps facing severe food insecurity across Karenni State.

  • Rice bags (primary staple)
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and split beans
  • Targeted distribution to female-headed households

Shelter & Emergency Logistics

Protecting displaced families from harsh weather, military operations, and seasonal flooding.

  • Tarpaulins for emergency shelters
  • Winter blankets for vulnerable families
  • Construction materials for storm repairs
  • Transportation for aid delivery

Medical Support & Health

Essential health interventions for communities with no access to clinics or medical facilities.

  • Mosquito nets for malaria prevention
  • Basic medicines and antibiotics
  • Emergency medical supply runs
  • Support for mobile health clinics

Active Distribution Network

Our logistical pipelines operate across highly remote regions including Nyaung Shwe, Kalaw, Taunggyi, Mese, Demoso, Bawlake, Shadaw, Hpruso, Loikaw, and Pekhon townships — reaching communities that no other organization currently serves.

Your Privacy Matters

Our Commitment to Donor Safety

Given the sensitive operational environment in which we work, protecting our donors is not just a policy — it is a responsibility we take seriously.

Confidential Payments

All payment channels are handled through trusted partners and secure banking systems to shield transactions from third-party interference.

Anonymity Safeguards

Your personal details, contact data, and funding amounts are strictly confidential and will never be shared with external or third-party authorities.

No Location Exposure

We do not publish exact camp coordinates, village tract names, or live route maps to protect our field operations and the communities we serve.

"Your personal details, contact data, and funding amounts are strictly confidential and will never be shared with external or third-party authorities, including government bodies or law enforcement agencies of any jurisdiction. CTER is committed to protecting the safety of every person who supports our mission."
