Who We Are

Born from Crisis, Built on Solidarity

The Coordination Team for Emergency Relief (Karenni), widely known as CTER – Karenni, was officially formed on March 9, 2021. Established by a dedicated network of local volunteers and diaspora members, CTER emerged in direct response to the severe humanitarian crisis triggered by the Myanmar military coup.

Our primary mission is to stabilize immediate food security and provide critical life-saving resources—including emergency food items, humanitarian shelter, and basic medical supplies—to Internally Displaced Persons (IDPs) and disaster-affected communities throughout Karenni State and neighboring regions. Concurrently, we advocate for the protection and human rights of the Karenni people on global platforms.

Core Principles

To preserve local trust and meet international standards, our operational actions strictly adhere to key humanitarian frameworks:

  • Do No Harm: Ensuring that our presence and delivery processes do not inadvertently create or exacerbate local vulnerabilities.
  • Inclusion: Prioritizing aid distribution equitably based on need, with specific focus on vulnerable households, children, the elderly, and persons with disabilities (PWDs).
  • Duty of Care: Safeguarding the security, physical safety, and dignity of both our field staff and the beneficiaries we serve.
